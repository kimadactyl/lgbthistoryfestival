The First National Festival of LGBT History

12th - 17th February 2015 – Hosted by the City of Manchester

Friday

  • Civic Launch at Manchester Town Hall

    Professor Charles Upchurch will deliver his ground-braking reserach on the first LGBT civil rights movement in the USA (?) in 1820. Mingle with the gerat and the good. Lord Mayor and Councillors in attendence.

    • Film

    Drei (2010, Germany, Tom Tykwer)

    Tom Tykwer's film deals with the changing nature of relationships and has an interesting take on the fluidity of sexuality. Andrew Moore will introduce the film and lead a discussuin afterwards.

    Starts 19.0 at Cruz 101
