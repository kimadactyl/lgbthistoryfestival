The First National Festival of LGBT History

12th - 17th February 2015 – Hosted by the City of Manchester

What's this?

LGBT History Month is in its 10th year, is celebrating with the First National Festival of LGBT History. The Festival has two main components: "What Is & to Do LGBT History?", an academic conference hosted at the Lesbian & Gay Foundation, and a main festival hosted at Central Library, The People's History Museum, and other prestigious venues around Manchester City Center.

Find out more about the festival …

Call for Contributions

We are pleased to invite members of the public, charities, NGOs and other LGBT and history organisations, to submit proposals for talks, films, and artworks, based on archival material. We seek to showcase and celebrate endeavours to illuminate the UK's LGBT past, and help write a richer and more inclusive history of Britain.
